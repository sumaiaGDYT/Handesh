Baking

Baking is my passion

I love to bake any pastries/sweets but mostly brownies. Baking or even being in the kitchen at my house is more than just the ingredients or utensils. I use it as a way of spending time with my sisters, mom, and aunts. My sisters are younger than me and not going to lie it gets a bit infuriating when the middle sister tells me what to do, but it's good. When I was younger I used to watch my mom and aunts make handesh also known as 'teler pithas'. They are a sweet treat which can be eaten as a snack. I was introduced to baking sweet treats/snacks when I was in elementary school. My mom and aunts would make handesh for the EID after Ramadan.

I would secretly eat the raw mix of the handesh, and it started when I was a toddler and my second aunt would feed me raw mix. I have sweet tooths and it taste really good. My mom and aunt inspire and still inspire me to bake mostly because of the time we spend baking treats/snacks. We would start with a normal conversation about literally anything and it would smoothy shift to when I was younger or my siblings/cousins and what we would do, then to when they were younger. I love listening to their stories from 'back in their day'. To the present day, whenever I get stuck I would ask my mom for help and after she's done helping me; she would tell me how I relate to her siblings when they were in their late teenage to mid adult years. Like I wrote before, I use baking as a way spending time with my family to hear their stories and learning more about them than what I already know.
